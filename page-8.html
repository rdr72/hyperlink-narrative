One thing I also remember vividly was trying to achieve my senior red belt while at the second school. Most belt tests for me were a one and done, except for senior red belt. This one belt took me three attempts to pass and the thing that made it worse was that there was a two month gap between every belt test, meaning it took me a total of six months to actually pass and receive the belt. In doing so, I received a plaque for having great perseverance despite the multiple setbacks and was also invited to be a part of the leadership team at that school.page 9
