After a four year break, I returned to taekwondo at the age of thirteen where I continued from blue tip all the way up to a 2nd degree black belt. The reason I started back at blue tip is because the first and third schools are olympic style taekwondo and the second school was American based. This mean the belt system was slightly different so when I joined the second school, they put me at the belt level that would correlate with where I sat in the first school. Page 7
